Design our homepage 2020!

As you can see our homepage is pretty bland at the moment. We're looking toward the community to design us a new homepage!

We have provided you with a few html elements that we want you to style using css3. Due to security concerns we will unfortunately not allow you to edit the HTML directly.

You can either copy the HTML source of this page into your own site and design your submission there or we alternatively provide a playground where you can design your page on our website. (For legal reasons we can only give you the ability to preview designs in the playground. You will still need to host your submission on your own website.)

Once you are happy with your page you can submit a link to the judges for rating. Submissions that gain a 10/10 rating will be given a secret invitation code to join the next round of the competition. We are focused on initial impact in this competition, and as such judges will only spend a maximum of 10 seconds on each submission.
